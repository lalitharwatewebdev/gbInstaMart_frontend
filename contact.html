Frequently asked questions

How do I register?

The registration process is quite easy; all you have to do is click on the "Sign In" section located at the top right corner of the home page. Please insert all the details in the form and click on submit. To verify your number, we will send OTP. Once verified, you can start shopping.

Is it necessary to register to shop from GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED?

However, you can browse through app GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED without registration. But to place an order, it's just to register.

Can I use the same email/phone number to register for multiple consumer accounts?

Each number and email belongs to any one consumer account.

How can I find a specific product?

You can browse it in various category pages or use a search bar at the top.

How do we ensure the quality and freshness of the product?

At GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED, we ensure that every product is handled and stored hygienically and at the correct temperature.

How to know if a product is available?

You can add all the items (willing to buy) to your cart and then put the address; the product's availability majorly depends on the location. If any product is unavailable, it will be notified as out of stock.

How do I place my order correctly?

Once your order is done, you will get a confirmation email and SMS containing all your order details.

Can I make changes in order?

No, you can't make modifications after placing an order. However, you can cancel it or ask for a refund as per the cancellation and refund policy.

Can I order on call?

We need the facility of taking orders on call.

What are the various modes of payment I can use for shopping?

At GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED, we accept various payment modes for our customers' smooth shopping experience.

The following payment modes are:

  • UPI
  • Credit Card / Debit Card
  • Net Banking
  • e-Wallets
  • Meal Cards
  • Pay Later
  • Cash on Delivery
Can I change the payment method later?

No, you can't change the payment method.

Can I pay online for the delivery of the product?

You can pay by scanning the QR on the invoice using any UPI app at delivery time.

How long does it take to get a refund?

It takes around 7 business days to get the refund on your canceled order.

How to get a refund for the order placed with cash on delivery?

The refund on your order placed with cash on delivery will be credited to your wallet, which can be later used for further orders.

What are RBI guidelines?

Per the updated RBI guidelines (effective 1st October 2022), merchants cannot save their debit or credit card details. However, the card will be saved at the merchant and payment gateway as a token.

Do I need to provide consent separately for each card?

Yes, per the latest RBI guidelines, you have to be permitted to save every card separately.

Can I delete the tokenized card?

Yes, you can delete any tokenized card with us.

Do I need any additional steps to tokenize the card or make a payment through a tokenized card?

No, there is no need to perform any extra steps. But to save the card in tokenized form, you must consent. The payment experience will be the same.

Do I have to pay extra charges to save or tokenize any card?

No, you don't need to charge extra for saving and tokenizing cards.

How will I know if GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED delivers to my area?

To know if GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED delivers to your area or not, you have to check the serviceability of your area, then select your PIN code and place the order.

How does GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED calculate the delivery fee?

Delivery fee for any order is calculated depending on the order value and product category/type.

Why do I see a delivery fee in my cart?

For orders below Rs 250, we have Rs. 40. However, we are not charging fees for orders above Rs 250.

Will I get delivery fees along with the refund?

For the cancellation of the order, you will get delivery fees back and a refund.

What's the order value to avail of free delivery?

To avail free delivery, you have to order worth Rs250.

Suppose I place multiple orders individually within a specific timeframe (3 hours), and the order value is less than Rs. 250 for every order. Can I get a waiver on the delivery fee?

It will only work when your order is worth less than Rs 250.

Would I get the delivery fee back if my product is damaged, the wrong item, etc., and in the non-returnable category?

The delivery fee will not be given back, whether it's a partial or complete order cancellation because it's the charge for the service which is already given.

Why am I unable to log in?

You may have entered the wrong details. Try again and make sure to put all the correct information. If the issue still needs to be solved, please email- Info@GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED.in or call us at +91 9920009909.

What's the difference between deactivating and deleting my account?

If you deactivate your account, you can reactivate your account again whenever you want. All the information is safe with us; whenever you reactivate your account, the information will be restored.

How do I deactivate my account?

All you have to do is write an email to us at Info@GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED.in with the subject line "Account deactivation". And also mention the authentic reason for deleting the account. Our team will get back to you on the matter, and the account will be deactivated within 15 days.

How do you reactivate the account?

It's easy to reactivate the account; write the email at Info@GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED.in with the subject line "Account Reactivation". Moreover, you have mentioned the associated mobile number. Our team will contact you to understand your matter and resolve it within 15 days. After reactivation, your account details will be restored.

What should customers do if they have become a victim of any fraud?

Our valuable customers are advised to report suspicious activity to authorities. Any consumer or person dealing with such fraudsters will be handling all matters at their risk and responsibility. GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED has not any responsibility for your loss.

What are how fraudsters may approach customers?
