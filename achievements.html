Our Achievements

It is our privilege to share our growth story – the success of GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED is driven by the commitment to deliver quality, superior value-added products and FMCG and grocery services. For the past couple of years, Findus has consistently demonstrated its dedication towards quality work, resultant customer satisfaction, and eco-friendly policies, for which it has been duly rewarded and achieved many milestones. All these achievements are a result of hard work, commitment, customer and partner satisfaction with our services.

This year, one of our most notable honours is the latest achievement of our company’s founder, Jayanti Lal Mali, granted the prestigious Maharashtra Udyog Gaurav Puraskar. This recognition is in acknowledgment of his outstanding contributions to the industry, great leadership, and sheer dedication to championing sustainable change in the FMCG and grocery market.

Under his guidance, GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED has:

  • Championed the setting of new standards in product quality as well as packaging design.
  • Launched a fully automated state-of-the-art manufacturing plant equipped with advanced technology.
  • Developed an easy-to-use application to make it accessible to all the regions in the country and ease its delivery.

These milestones, our years of experience, the warm relationship with customers, and the quality of services we offer have helped us carve a niche in the market. Both recognitions inspire us to enhance our mission to bring innovation and quality to the everyday products used in Indian homes.

We are still aspiring to reach even higher, provide nothing less than super excellent services, and continue to write the next chapter of the FMCG and groceries business in India.
