GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED - Manufacturers

GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED, operating under the trademark ZENZAIKA foods and spices pvt. ltd., is a prominent player in the FMCG and grocery sector based in Mumbai. As a manufacturer, we stand out for our extensive range of products that cater to diverse culinary needs across India. With a focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED has established itself as a trusted name in the industry.

Product Range

  • Masalas: Range of products include spices necessary in Indian recipes. ZENZAIKA foods and spices pvt. ltd. is an enabler to various cuisines, offering every type of masala from garam masala to specialities like pav bhaji masala and biryani masala.
  • Nimkos: Best taken with tea or as a simple snack. We have a variety of nimkos such as crispy sev, spicy chakli, and crunchy mathri, all using high-quality ingredients for a fresh and yummy taste.
  • Pickles and Chutneys: A variety of pickles and chutneys to complement meals, widely preferred by buyers.
  • Ready-to-Cook Mixes: Convenient products for health-conscious individuals who want to save time, offering spices and meals in one package.

Quality Assurance

GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED values quality as emphasized in our corporate vision and mission statement. Raw materials are specially procured from suppliers who strictly meet the proposed quality standards. We conduct tests during manufacturing to ensure that our products meet strict requirements for taste, aroma, and freshness.

Expert Team

Providing White Labelling Services

In addition to manufacturing for our own brand, ZENZAIKA foods and spices pvt. ltd., GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED also offers white labelling services for other companies and startups in the FMCG industry.

Advantages of White Labelling for Startups and Companies

  • Cost-Effective Solution: White labelling allows startups to enter the market without bearing the high costs of establishing production facilities.
  • Access to Quality Products: Startups gain access to high-quality products produced under strict standards, boosting confidence in the market.
  • Faster Time-to-Market: With pre-existing formulations and packaging, startups can introduce new products quickly, saving time and resources.
  • Customization Options: Businesses can adjust formulations and packaging to meet specific needs, helping them differentiate from competitors.
  • Focus on Branding: White labelling allows companies to concentrate on building their brand and marketing rather than production logistics.

Benefits for GROWBIG SUPERMARTS LIMITED

  • Increased Production Volume: White labelling increases production volumes, boosting sales and profitability without the need to acquire new customers.
  • Enhanced Market Reach: Through collaborations with different brands, we can enter new markets, expanding our reach.
  • Brand Diversification: White labelling allows us to expand our product line, ensuring long-term business stability.
  • Strengthened Relationships: Working with other companies fosters valuable partnerships that open doors for future collaborations and projects.
